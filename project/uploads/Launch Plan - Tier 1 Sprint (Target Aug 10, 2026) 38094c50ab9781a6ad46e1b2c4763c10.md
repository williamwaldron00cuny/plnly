# Launch Plan — Tier 1 Sprint (Target Aug 10, 2026)

> **Target: Tier 1 live by Monday, August 10, 2026 — 8 weeks out.** Tier 1 = live one-page site with online booking, marketing materials in place, and a marketing plan. This date is a target to react to, not a promise — two pieces (entity filing, insurance underwriting) run on timelines neither of us controls, so the plan flexes around those.
> 

## Decisions needed now — nothing here is assumed

**Business entity structure.** Sole proprietorship + DBA vs. forming an LLC in New York — different cost, paperwork, and liability profiles, and NY has its own quirks (including LLC-specific filing requirements). Worth a dedicated research session plus likely a short accountant consult before anything gets filed. Flagging now so it isn't the bottleneck when the bank account is needed in Week 4.

**Liability insurance.** Going into clients' homes and touching their calendars, accounts, and household-admin tools means general liability — and possibly professional liability — isn't optional. Needs a broker conversation, and underwriting takes time, so starting early matters.

**Website platform.** Two real options: (1) Wix with the AI site builder — faster to a working site, less granular design control; (2) a custom-built site — full control to match the brand guide exactly, more build time. Decide once the brand direction is visible.

**Domain + social handles.** [plnly.co](http://plnly.co) and [plnly.nyc](http://plnly.nyc) — both purchased via Spaceship, $8.86 total (receipt EH-202606162100383, June 16, 2026). Social handles (Instagram, TikTok, Threads, LinkedIn at minimum) still need checking + claiming on Billy's end.

**Sub-brand naming + pronunciation.** Resolved — name is locked as PLNLY (written/stylized) with "Plainly" as the spoken name and the logo treatment: "Plainly" sits under the PLNLY wordmark as a pronunciation hint, by design rather than as a fallback. What the college-prep AI-literacy offering is called is still open — slotted into Week 2.
