## PLNLY Travel

### The AI plans the trip. You still make every call.

Most of what eats a weekend before a trip isn't the traveling — it's the forty open tabs: routes, stays, the restaurant that books out three weeks ahead, the flight that's quietly cheaper on a Tuesday. PLNLY Travel is two ways to make that part disappear without handing over control of your own trip.

### Two ways to travel well

**Build it with us.** As part of any PLNLY setup, we configure the AI that plans and re-plans your travel — itineraries, booking logic, packing, real-time changes when a flight moves — around how your family actually travels. You leave owning the workflow, not renting it. It's the household service, pointed at the airport.

**Or have it handed to you.** Some trips you don't want to build — you just want the finished plan. That's **Network Planning**, our travel studio: deeply researched field guides with day-by-day routing, vetted stays and tables, cost ranges, and booking links, plus a Notion companion layer. You book on your own time; they did every hour of research it takes to make that easy.

### Three ways in

- **Curated AI Travel Tools — $150.** A PLNLY package on its own: we set up the AI travel tools around your trip and your travel style — flight and itinerary planning, live rebooking, packing, the logistics — then hand you the keys, same as everything else PLNLY builds.
- **Network Planning Field Guide — from $39.** The research, done for you, at Network Planning's own prices (Digital from $39; Print + Digital from $69; bespoke Custom Dossier quoted). Add it to any PLNLY package.
- **Field Guide + Curated AI Travel Tools — $150 on top of the guide.** Both together: the finished plan, plus the AI set up to run with it.
