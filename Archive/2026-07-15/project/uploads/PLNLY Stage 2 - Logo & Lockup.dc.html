06 — Built for a family

Here's the clever part you asked for. PLNLY is "plainly" with the A and the I removed — modern life, minus the jargon, and quietly minus the "AI." The first sub-brand — the AI-literacy service, which won't carry a separate name — is the one place we put those two letters back, lit in coral.
