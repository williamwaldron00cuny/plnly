# Visual Identity — Brand Guidelines v1.0

> The living system behind PLNLY. Every item here is a rule meant to be applied across web, social, and print — not a picture to admire. Companion files: Framer Build Guide · Claude Code Handoff.
> 

## 01 · Strategy & Positioning

**An ownership brand, not a concierge.** Everyone else runs the AI and keeps the keys. We set the tools up, teach you to run them, and *hand over the keys*.

- **Brand promise:** Modern life, minus the jargon.
- **Tagline:** Built bespoke. Yours to run.
- **Audiences:** (1) busy, affluent households who want to own the capability, not outsource it; (2) parents readying college-bound teens for responsible AI-tool literacy.
- **Personality:** warm but not eager · a little mysterious · competent, never patronizing · quietly high-end · above the fray.

## 02 · Logo & Lockup

The wordmark **PLNLY** never appears without *plainly* beneath it on first contact. The divider sits equidistant between the two. The coral dot is the brand's standalone device.

- **Wordmark:** Outfit Medium, uppercase, letter-spacing .045em.
- **Reveal:** *plainly* in Newsreader Italic, coral, letter-spacing ~.16em.
- **Variations:** stacked (primary) · horizontal · compact "P." mark · the coral dot · reversed on ink.
- **Clear space:** equal to the cap-height of the "P" on all sides.
- **Minimum size:** 22px wide on screen, 9mm in print. Below that, drop the reveal and use the compact mark.
- **Never:** recolor the wordmark · stretch it · set *plainly* upright · separate the two at first contact.

## 04 · Typography

Four voices, each used once per composition.

- **Outfit** — display & headlines, the wordmark. Weights 400/500. Never bold.
- **Newsreader (italic)** — the one elevated word; the reveal. Italic 300/400, usually coral.
- **Hanken Grotesk** — body & interface workhorse. 16/1.6, measure 45–75 characters.
- **IBM Plex Mono** — labels, eyebrows, data. Uppercase, wide tracking.

**Scale:** Display 60 · H1 44 · H2 32 · H3 24 · Lead 19 · Body 16 · Small 14 · Caption 12 (Mono, .16em caps).

*Rule: one Newsreader italic moment per screen — two is one too many. Never set a full sentence in the italic.*

## 05 · Spacing & Grid

- **Base unit: 8** (with 4 as a half-step). Everything snaps to it.
- **Scale:** 4 · 8 · 12 · 16 · 24 · 32 · 48 · 64 · 96 · 128. Use 4–32 inside components, 48–64 between blocks, 96–128 between sections.
- **Grid:** 12 columns, 24px gutter, max 1080–1120px, auto margins. Responsive 4 → 8 → 12.
- **Radius:** 2 (chips) · 4–6 (cards) · 12 (tiles) · pill (buttons). Mostly square.
- **Elevation:** soft and low — raised (Y1 blur3 6%), floating, overlay. Never dramatic.

## 06 · Icon, Imagery & Motif

- **Icons:** abstract before literal, built from dot / ring / line. 24px grid, 1.75px stroke, round caps. Coral in at most one element, only when it means something.
- **The quiet orbit:** concentric hairline rings with a coral center (household) and mint/mist satellites. The cosmic, iykyk nod that replaces every AI cliché. Use large & faint as a backdrop, small as a section marker, on ink as the social avatar, or as a calm processing state.
- **Imagery:** warm, real, unhurried · soft natural light · objects & hands over faces · generous negative space.
- **Avoid:** robots · circuits · neon · stock "tech" · cold blue gradients.
- **Texture:** flat on screen; tactile uncoated stock in print.

## 07 · Motion & Voice

**Motion** — arrive, don't perform; slow and sure; once is enough.

- Durations: 160ms micro · 320ms standard · 700ms the reveal.
- Easing: cubic-bezier(.22,.61,.36,1), no overshoot. Respect prefers-reduced-motion.
- The signature: the wordmark is present first, then the divider and *plainly* rise gently in beneath it.

**Voice** — capable not reassuring · warm with edges · one elevated word · specific over generic.

- ✓ "Here's the setup. You've got it from here." · "Built bespoke. Yours to run."
- ✕ "Don't worry — it's so easy!" · "Unlock the power of AI to transform your life."

## 08 · The Family & PLAINLY

PLNLY is "plainly" with the **A** and the **I** removed — modern life, minus the jargon, and quietly minus the "AI." The first sub-brand (the AI-literacy program, which won't carry a separate name) is the one place we put them back, lit in coral: **PL·AI·NLY**.

The umbrella endorses from above; each future property keeps its own front door and, eventually, its own secondary hue from the palette. Shared DNA: the geometric wordmark, the coral *plainly*, the dot, the orbit. Naming for future named lines is deferred per the brief — nothing invented ahead of it.
