PLNLY · Visual Identity — Stage 05

Spacing & grid.

The invisible part — and the one that makes the difference between "calm and intentional" and "templated." Everything is built on an 8-point rhythm, laid on a 12-column grid, with generous air around it. Space is the brand's loudest material: when in doubt, add room, not ornament.

01 — The base unit
8px
Everything snaps to 8.

Padding, gaps, margins, and component heights are all multiples of 8 — with 4 available as a half-step for tight spots (icon gaps, chip insets). One rhythm across every surface is what makes a card, a slide, and a business card feel like one family. No arbitrary 13s or 27s.

Inside components use 4–32. Between blocks use 48–64. Between major sections use 96–128. The big steps are where the calm comes from — don't be afraid of them.
