PLNLY · Visual Identity — Stage 01

Three visual territories

Before any logo is finalized, three complete little worlds. Each is faithful to the brief — greige ground, layered neutrals, one sparing accent — but each sets a different temperature. Don't judge them as finished logos; judge the feeling. Tell me which one's air you want to breathe, and we refine from there. You can also take the accent from one and the type from another — that's the point of doing it this way.
